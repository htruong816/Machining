Project Objective

Part function, skills, and theory demonstrated

What this part does

The drill drift is used to remove tapered shank tools (like a Morse taper drill) from a spindle or sleeve by delivering a controlled wedge action and transferring force safely.

  • Function: Eject tapered tool from taper socket
  • Performance target: Strong, straight, no burrs on striking/wedge surfaces
  • Constraints: Must fit the taper slot and withstand impact
